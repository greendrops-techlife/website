About

A weaving mill built by weavers.

Founded in 2014, Greendrops Techlife is a Coimbatore-based manufacturer of industrial woven fabric and preparatory yarn. We run our own warping and twisting lines so we control quality from yarn to finished roll.

Customers buy from us when specification matters — filter fabrics, coir fiber sheets, and technical cotton/rayon weaves that need consistent sett, tension, and hand.

Capabilities

Four disciplines, one roof.

Every step from raw yarn to finished fabric is handled in-house. Own the preparatory, own the quality.

01 · Weaving

Industrial fabric weaving

Heavy-duty woven fabrics for filtration, technical, and process-industry use. Nylon, polycotton, cotton, and rayon woven to spec on GSM, sett, width, and weave structure — plain, twill, satin, and specialty patterns.

  • Nylon & polycotton weaving for industrial applications
  • Filter fabric for dewatering and chemical filtration
  • Technical fabric for process industries
  • Width-to-order, tight GSM tolerance
02 · Cotton & Rayon

Cotton & rayon weaving

Grey and finished cotton, rayon, and blended fabrics. Soft hand, consistent shrinkage, predictable dye pickup. Woven to your construction sheet — count, reed, pick, and cover.

  • 100% cotton, 100% rayon, and blended constructions
  • Plain, twill, and specialty weaves
  • Grey fabric or finished rolls
03 · Warping

Warping

Direct and sectional warping on precision beams. Uniform tension across the full warp sheet means clean sheds, fewer breaks, and faster loom speeds downstream.

  • Direct warping for production runs
  • Sectional warping for small lots and stripes
  • Beam-to-loom ready, job-work for outside mills
04 · Twisting

Twisting

Two-for-one and ring twisting for single and plied yarn. Custom twists-per-metre (TPM) per customer recipe — for strength, lustre, or specialty yarn builds.

  • Single and 2-ply, 3-ply constructions
  • S-twist and Z-twist to spec
  • Tight TPM control, lot-to-lot consistency
